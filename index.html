What is this book about?
PrimeFaces Cookbook covers over 100 effective recipes to PrimeFaces 3.x which this leading component suite offers you to boost JSF applications - from AJAX basics, theming and input components to advanced usage of datatable, menus, drag & drop and charts. Inclusive writing of custom components and PrimeFaces Extensions.

Authors
